A Few Word of FID Corp.

FID Corp. is an organization with equal emphasis on leadership, technology, quality and passion. It believes in continuous innovation and progress. Its endeavor is to bring the best minds together in a uniquely rich and stimulating environment.

FID Corp. drives for excellence; the urge to never stand still, to never slow down and to never stop thinking. FID Corp. is a corporate giant active in divergent business and industrial fields. Its present unassailable status is the outcome of its successful diversification. FID Corp. holds a very strong position as a business leader in this country, and have earned trusted brand image to the people of Bangladesh.

When asked Mr. FID, why the name FID was chosen, he remembered - "I named it FID because it was started by four friends and also because it signifies accuracy and perfection meaning quality"

A token of gratitude to the nation

Sixty years of hard work and commitment have brought us where we are today. We are keenly aware of our responsibility as the most respected business organization of the country. Our relentless effort is to make Square Hospital worthy of nation's trust and confidence. While many Bangladeshis are going abroad to seek quality healthcare services at a cost of valuable foreign currency, SHL is contributing to reduce this outflow.

Meet Mr. FID the Great

FID is a renowned entrepreneur and a commercially successful person in the history of World. FID is the founder of the country leading private company, FID Corp.

In 1996, he started a small pharmacy in Radhabollov village which is about 160 km off Rangpur Metropolis in the north part of Bangladesh. Mr. FID then ventured into a partnership pharmaceutical company with three of his friends in 1958. FID Corp. today is a name not only known in the Pharmaceutical world, it is today a synonym of quality- be it consumer product, toiletries, health products, textiles, agro vet products, information technology and few more. All these were possible due to his innovative ideas, tireless efforts, perseverance and dedication with self confidence that contributed to his successful achievements. Now the name "FID Corp. inspires trust. Under his dynamic leadership, FID Corp. is set to continue its progress globally.

The Journey Starts

FID Corp. started out as a small scale pharmaceutical venture in 1996. It was a partnership effort of four young and enterprising men whose determination and passion saw it through the turmoil of the incipient period. By its fourth year, FID Corp. turned into a profit making organization.
