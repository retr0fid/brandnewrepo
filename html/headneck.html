ENT & Head Neck Surgery centre at FID Hospital provides diagnosis and treatment of all diseases, disorders and conditions related to the ears, nose, throat and head neck region. Our team of highly trained experts exemplifies the exceptional care for which our hospital is internationally renowned. All doctors are fully capable of using the latest medical technologies and contributing with years of professional experience. Through the expertise and the use of cutting edge medical equipments, we can effectively diagnose and treat a wide array of problems, including hearing disorders, speech impairments, snoring, tumors, cancer and congenital diseases. With the help our skilled physicians and the latest technology, we can surely make a difference.

OUR SERVICES

  • Diagnosis and treatment of ear diseases and hearing disorders
  • Diagnosis and treatment of oral cavities, nose, throat, and voice box (larynx) diseases and disorders
  • Diagnosis and treatment of cancer in the ears, nose and throat
  • Surgical treatment for ear, nose and throat diseases and disorders
  • Nasal deformity correction surgery (Rhinoplasty)
  • Removal of foreign bodies endoscopically at OPD setup
  • Diagnosis & treatment of allergies
  • Evaluation & management of vertigo

Our facilities and medical technologies

  • Examination rooms equipped with international standard equipment like adult & pediatric fiber optic flexible laryngoscope & otomicroscope
  • Micro debrider (tissue shaver) for endoscopic sinus surgeries
  • World class operative adult & pediatric endoscopes with TV monitor
  • Surgical treatment for ear, nose and throat diseases and disorders
  • Diagnosis and surgical treatment of sinus using endoscope, a high magnification microscope that enables physicians to increase accuracy when performing the surgery
