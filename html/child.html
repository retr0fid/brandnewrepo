FID Child Development Centre (FCDC)

Our motto is to prevent disability, optimize development and improve quality of survival of all children in Bangladesh. To establish child and family-friendly FID Child Developmental Centre (FCDC) we apply strategies and tools standardized for Bangladeshi children for early screening, assessment, intervention, treatment and management of the entire range of neurodevelopmental disorders and disabilities likeo

Development Delays
  • Cerebral Palsy
  • Autism spectrum disorders
  • Epilepsy
  • Hyperactive Child
  • Psychosocial issues
  • School refusal, temper tantrum, conduct disorder, OCD
We provide psychosocial services to families and empower parents and primary care-providers to optimize their child’s development.
